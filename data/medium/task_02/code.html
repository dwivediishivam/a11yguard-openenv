Classic Margherita Pizza

Prep: 30 min · Cook: 12 min · Serves: 4

Ingredients

  • 2½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ¾ cup warm water
  • ½ cup San Marzano tomato sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves

Instructions

  1. Combine flour, yeast, and salt in a bowl. Add water and olive oil. Knead for 8 minutes until smooth.
  2. Cover dough and let it rise for 1 hour in a warm place.
  3. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C) with a pizza stone if available.
  4. Stretch dough into a 12-inch circle on a floured surface.
  5. Spread tomato sauce evenly, leaving a 1-inch border.
  6. Top with mozzarella slices.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove from oven, top with fresh basil, and serve immediately.
